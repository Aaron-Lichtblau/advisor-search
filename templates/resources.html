Resources


It is best to get started on your Independent Work or Thesis as early as possible. Check out these resources to get started early, find funding, and stay up to date with the schedule of deadlines.


Funding Options:


AB students can apply for IW project/senior thesis research funding by accessing the online application in the Student Activities Funding Engine (SAFE). Please refer to the Office of Undergraduate Research calendar for deadline dates for funding.

BSE students will receive an email from the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences regarding funding at the beginning of the semester. The deadline date for Fall 2019 is TBD, but you can expect it to be in mid-October. Also, here is the link to the SEAS funding website.

IW Students are also encouraged to review the Independent Work Equipment List for materials for their upcooming project. All equipment/materials must be returned at the conclusion of the project.

This is the current SEAS funding application. Be sure to look it over and know what to expect when applying for funding.

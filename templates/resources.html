Getting Started


It is best to get started with finding your advisor as early as possible. Once you have found a professor whose work excites you, go ahead and send an email expressing your interest in being their advisee. Here is an example email for you to build off:


Dear Professor _______,


I am a junior/senior in the Computer Science department and I am exploring areas of research to do my thesis/IW work. In this search process, I reviewed past theses/IW projects in AreasOfReasearch: _______. One paper that fascinated me was (Title of Project) by (author of IW/thesis name).
